Iconic Moments
  • 🏆 1989 — Senna vs Prost I: The chicane collision that defined an era. Prost walked away champion after Senna was disqualified for rejoining via the escape road. Politics, rage, and the greatest rivalry F1 has ever seen.
  • 💥 1990 — Senna vs Prost II: Senna deliberately drove into Prost at Turn 1. "If he thinks I'm going to leave room, he's wrong." Title settled on the spot. The most premeditated move in F1 history.
  • 🌧️ 2005 — Räikkönen from P17: Kimi started 17th and carved through the entire field, passing Fisichella on the final lap for the win. Many consider it the greatest drive in Suzuka history.
  • 🇯🇵 2012 — Kobayashi's home podium: Kamui Kobayashi P3 for Sauber — first Japanese driver on the Suzuka podium in 22 years. The crowd went absolutely mental.
  • 😢 2014 — Jules Bianchi: Rain, a recovery crane, and an impact that changed F1 safety forever. Bianchi passed away 9 months later. The halo exists because of Suzuka.
  • 🏎️ 2000 — Schumacher's redemption: After 21 years, Ferrari won the drivers' championship again. Schumacher cried on the podium. Five consecutive titles started here.
  • 🎌 The circuit itself: Designed by John Hugenholtz in 1962 as Honda's test track. The only figure-8 circuit in F1. The S-curves, 130R, Spoon, and Degner — every corner has a story. Drivers consistently rate it the most rewarding track to drive.
