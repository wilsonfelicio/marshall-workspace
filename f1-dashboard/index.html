Iconic Moments
  • 🏆 2022 — Verstappen's inaugural win: Started P3, overtook Leclerc and Sainz to win the first-ever Miami GP. Late safety car made for a tense finish, but Max held firm. The beginning of a new classic.
  • 🚀 2023 — Verstappen from P9: After a difficult qualifying, Max carved through the entire field from ninth to win. Legendary tire management and pace. Set the lap record (1:29.708) in the process.
  • 🎉 2024 — Norris's maiden win: After 110 races, Lando finally broke through. Started P5, benefited from a safety car and the upgraded McLaren's raw pace. Emotional scenes — the monkey was off his back.
  • 🔧 2025 — McLaren 1-2, Piastri dominates: Oscar Piastri led a McLaren 1-2 with an immaculate drive. The team executed a flawless double-stack pit stop. McLaren's third consecutive win — a dynasty forming.
  • 🏟️ The venue: Built around Hard Rock Stadium, home of the Miami Dolphins. The faux marina with yachts, the celebrity paddock, the neon-drenched atmosphere — love it or hate it, Miami is F1's most American race. The track punishes cars with extreme heat and humidity.
  • 💨 3 DRS zones = chaos: The triple-DRS setup creates some of the best wheel-to-wheel racing on the calendar. The T11 and T17 braking zones are where races are won and lost. Combined zones 2 & 3 allow multi-corner attack sequences like Jeddah.

📰 Key Stories Heading Into Miami

  • Antonelli leads the championship — youngest ever WDC leader at 19. Two consecutive wins (China, Japan). The kid is the real deal. Can Mercedes maintain dominance on a power-heavy street circuit?
  • 5-week break after Japan — Bahrain & Saudi cancelled due to Middle East conflict. Teams have had a full month to develop. Who brings the biggest upgrades to Miami?
  • McLaren's Miami fortress — back-to-back winners here (Norris '24, Piastri '25). Norris P5 with 25 pts, Piastri P6 with 21. Dangerous on a power circuit with 3 DRS zones.
  • Verstappen & Red Bull struggling — P9 in championship, only 12 pts after 3 races. Red Bull P6 in constructors tied with Alpine on 16 pts. The 2026 regs have exposed them. Must-improve weekend.
  • Bearman the surprise package — Haas rookie P7 with 17 pts, ahead of Gasly, Verstappen, and Lawson. Haas P4 in constructors with 18 pts. The new regs are a great equalizer.
  • Sprint format — extra points on offer Saturday. With only 1 practice session, preparation is compressed. Rewards drivers who adapt fast. Antonelli's natural speed vs Russell's methodical approach — advantage kid.
